ABOUT

Originally founded to handle production of Aniplex's family-themed series, the studio has grown and expanded as a studio that fully participates in anime production and other activities, which it holds. The studio has already developed an international presence, participating in the international convention, Anime Expo 2007 (AX 2007) held in ([Long Beach, California|Long Beach]], California, and having its own panel there.[5] In April 2018, lahuri-picture has changed the name of one of their studios, Kōenji Studio to CloverWorks, which is expected to become its own unique identity and distinguishable from its core studio, Asagaya Studio.
